# 01 — Vision

## Saarthi AI: A Goal-Oriented AI Operating System

> **Version**: 2.0  
> **Author**: Founding CTO  
> **Status**: Active

---

## The Problem We're Solving

People have goals. They lack:
- A system to structure goals into executable plans
- AI that adapts to *their* context — not generic advice
- One platform that spans Career, Learning, Research, Business, and Health
- A personal AI that grows smarter with every interaction

Most tools today are single-purpose widgets. Saarthi is the OS that powers all of them.

---

## The Vision

> **"Saarthi AI is a Goal-Oriented AI Operating System that helps people achieve what matters to them."**

It is **not** a chatbot. It is **not** a career website. It is **not** a productivity app.

It is the OS layer that coordinates AI agents, user memory, knowledge, and automation — all centered on **Goals**.

---

## North Star Metric

> How many goals did users fully complete this month?

Every engineering, product, and design decision maps back to this metric.

## Target Users (Year 1)

1. **Students** — Undergrad, MTech, PhD, MS Abroad
2. **Freshers** — Entering job market
3. **Professionals** — Career switching, upskilling
4. **Researchers** — Academic work, thesis management
5. **Builders** — Startup founders, indie hackers

---

## The 10-Year Picture

Saarthi becomes the default AI OS for personal and professional growth — like a combination of:

- A **personal chief of staff** powered by AI
- A **second brain** that remembers everything
- A **goal machine** that breaks down ambition into action

---

## Core Values

1. **Privacy First** — User data is never sold. Memory is owned by the user.
2. **Provider Agnostic** — Never tied to OpenAI, Google, or any single model.
3. **Offline Capable** — Can run with Ollama locally. No cloud required.
4. **Open Architecture** — Community modules and agents in the future.
5. **Radical Simplicity** — If the user has to think, we failed.
