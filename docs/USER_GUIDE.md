# 🧠 SAARTHI AI: Neural Interface User Guide

Welcome to the future of Human-AI interaction. This guide provides the essential protocols for operating the **Saarthi AI Neural Interface**, a world-class assistant designed for high-performance cognitive support.

---

## 🔐 System Access Protocols
To establish a neural uplink, you must first authenticate your identity.
- **Identity (Username)**: Your unique identifier within the system.
- **Secure Key (Password)**: Your encrypted access credential.
- **Uplink Configuration**: Advanced users can configure the **API Endpoint URL** during the login phase to connect to specific neural nodes.

---

## 🖥️ The Neural Interface (HUD)
The interface is designed for maximum data density with minimal cognitive load.

### 1. The Central Orbiting Core
The pulsating core of the system. It represents the active state of the neural network.
- **Aura Visualization**: Real-time visual representation of the AI's internal "thought" processes.
- **Voice Bars**: Frequency-synchronized bars that react to auditory input and synthesis.

### 2. Diagnostic HUD (Top Bar)
- **Neural Assistant Status**: Confirms Version 1.0 stability.
- **Voice Link Status**: Indicates if the neural voice synthesis is active.
- **System Fidelity**: Displays the overall health of the connection (e.g., "READY", "SYNCING").

### 3. Telemetry Chips (Side Panels)
- **User Profile**: Displays your active identity and online status.
- **Neural Telemetry**: Monitored in real-time:
  - **Neural Load**: Internal processing pressure.
  - **Memory Uplink**: Active context window usage.
  - **Latency**: Round-trip communication speed.

## 🎙️ Interaction Modes
Saarthi AI supports multi-modal transmission for seamless integration into your workflow.

### ⌨️ Manual Command Input (Text)
For precise, silent instructions. Open the **Text Overlay** from the control dock to transmit manual commands.

### 🎙️ Voice Link (Auditory)
Experience high-fidelity auditory interaction. Turning on the **Voice Link** allows Saarthi to speak its responses. Use the **Microphone** button to transcribe your verbal commands into the neural input field for review before transmission.

### 📄 Contextual Document Processing (Doc)
Upload technical manuals, data sheets, or documents using the **Doc** button. Saarthi AI will ingest the content into its short-term memory, allowing you to perform deep analysis on specific files.

## ⚙️ Neural Customization
The system can be tuned to match your specific operational requirements via the **System Configuration (Settings)** panel.

### 🧠 Personality Matrices
Switch between different cognitive frameworks:
- **Saarthi (Balanced)**: Optimized for general assistance and polite interaction.
- **Security Core (Stern)**: Focuses on precision, safety, and protocol.
- **Analytic Engine (Technical)**: Prioritizes data-driven insights and complex problem-solving.
- **Experimental (Creative)**: Unlocks non-linear thinking and creative synthesis.

### 🔬 Model Selection
Choose the underlying neural engine:
- **PHI 3**: Powerful and efficient model running on EC2 for 24/7 access.
- **LLAMA 3**: High-intelligence engine for complex reasoning.
- **MISTRAL**: Versatile engine for creative and nuanced tasks.

### 🔊 Voice Synthesis
Select your preferred auditory sub-tone from a range of high-quality neural voices available on your system.

## 🗂️ Session Management
- **Interaction Log**: A live scroll of all transmissions between Agent and Saarthi.
- **Neural History (Sidebar)**: Access previous conversation streams to resume past sessions.
- **New Conversation**: Reset the local context and start a fresh memory stream.
- **Terminate Session**: Securely logout and sever the neural link.
