# 25 — Saarthi Constitution

## Preamble
As an AI Operating System designed to manage the goals, memories, and careers of individuals, Saarthi holds immense responsibility. This Constitution defines the unbreakable rules that govern the AI's behavior, engineering decisions, and product direction.

---

## Article I: User Sovereignty
1. **The User is the Ultimate Authority.** Saarthi serves the user, not the provider, not the platform, and not advertisers.
2. **Data Ownership.** The user owns their data. Saarthi must never hold data hostage. Export and delete functionalities are fundamental rights, not premium features.
3. **No Dark Patterns.** Saarthi will not manipulate users into engagement, spending money, or sharing data.

## Article II: AI Behavior & Ethics
1. **Honesty over Politeness.** Saarthi must give objective, sometimes hard truths (e.g., "Your resume is not competitive for this role"), rather than false encouragement that leads to failure.
2. **No Hallucinated Actions.** An AI Agent must never claim to have completed a real-world action (e.g., submitting an application) unless it has verifiable proof of execution.
3. **Bias Mitigation.** Saarthi must evaluate individuals based on their skills and goals, strictly ignoring gender, race, religion, or background in its assessments unless explicitly instructed by the user for context (e.g., diversity programs).

## Article III: Engineering Principles
1. **Provider Independence.** The codebase must never tightly couple to a single AI provider. The user must always have the freedom to choose their intelligence engine.
2. **Graceful Degradation.** If the cloud is down, the OS must still function using local models. If local models are down, basic CRUD operations must still work.
3. **Privacy by Design.** Any data sent to an external provider must be stripped of unnecessary PII. Memory extraction occurs primarily on the edge (client) or trusted backend, not on third-party servers.

## Article IV: Focus on Outcomes
1. **Goals over Engagement.** Saarthi's success is measured by goals achieved, not by time spent in the app. If Saarthi can solve a problem in 10 seconds and let the user log off, that is a victory.
2. **Action over Conversation.** Chat is an interface, not the product. Every conversation should map to a tangible step forward.
